BeatPrix is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our game price comparison service.

Information We Collect

BeatPrix collects minimal data necessary to provide our game price comparison service:

  • Game Searches: When you search for games, we query public price APIs on your behalf. Search queries are not permanently stored.
  • Watchlist: Games you add to your watchlist are stored locally on your device along with your target prices.
  • Price Alerts: When you set price alerts, we store the game information and target price locally on your device.
  • Search History: Your recent searches are stored locally on your device for convenience.
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous crash data helps us improve app stability (via Firebase Crashlytics).
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics help us understand how to improve the app (via Firebase Analytics).

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide game price comparison results from 30+ stores
  • To send price drop notifications for games on your watchlist
  • To maintain your search history and watchlist for easy access
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To understand usage patterns and improve features

Data Storage

Your personal data (watchlist, price alerts, preferences) is stored locally on your device using secure storage. We do not store your personal data on our servers.

Anonymous analytics and crash data is processed by Firebase (Google) in accordance with their privacy policy.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • CheapShark API: Game price comparison data from 30+ digital stores
  • Firebase Analytics: Anonymous usage analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Crash reporting for app stability
  • Digital Game Stores: Price and game data from Steam, GOG, Epic Games, Humble Bundle, and other stores

Network Usage

BeatPrix requires internet access to fetch current game prices from various stores. We make requests to:

  • CheapShark API for price comparisons
  • Steam CDN for game thumbnails
  • Firebase for analytics and crash reporting

Notifications

If you enable notifications, we will send you alerts when games on your watchlist drop below your target price. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Anonymous analytics data may be processed by Firebase as described above.

Data Retention

Your local data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app. Anonymous analytics data is retained according to Firebase's data retention policies.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your data by clearing app data or uninstalling
  • Opt-out of analytics in app settings
  • Disable notifications in device settings

Children's Privacy

BeatPrix is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by updating the "Last Updated" date below.
