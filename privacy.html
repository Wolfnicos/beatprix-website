Beatprix is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Information We Collect

Beatprix collects minimal data necessary to provide our price comparison service:

  • Barcode Scans: Product barcodes you scan are processed to find product information and prices. This data is stored locally on your device.
  • Price Alerts: When you set price alerts, we store the product barcode and your target price locally on your device.
  • Scan History: Your scanning history is stored locally on your device for your convenience.
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous crash data helps us improve app stability (via Firebase Crashlytics).
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics help us understand how to improve the app (via Firebase Analytics).

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide price comparison results for scanned products
  • To send price alert notifications when prices drop
  • To maintain your scan history for easy access
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To understand usage patterns and improve features

Data Storage

Your personal data (scan history, price alerts, preferences) is stored locally on your device using secure storage. We do not store your personal data on our servers.

Anonymous analytics and crash data is processed by Firebase (Google) in accordance with their privacy policy.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Analytics: Anonymous usage analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Crash reporting for app stability
  • Open Food Facts: Product database for barcode lookups
  • Various Retailers: Price data from public retail websites

Camera Permission

Beatprix requires camera access solely for scanning product barcodes. We do not store, transmit, or process any images from your camera beyond the barcode scanning function.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, we will send you alerts when tracked product prices drop below your target. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Anonymous analytics data may be processed by Firebase as described above.

Data Retention

Your local data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app. Anonymous analytics data is retained according to Firebase's data retention policies.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your data by clearing app data or uninstalling
  • Opt-out of analytics in app settings
  • Disable notifications in device settings

Children's Privacy

Beatprix is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by updating the "Last Updated" date below.
