Frequently Asked Questions

How do I search for a game?

Open BeatPrix and use the search bar on the home screen to find any game. You can also scan the barcode on a physical game case to look up digital prices for that title.

Which stores does BeatPrix compare?

BeatPrix compares prices from 30+ digital stores including Steam, GOG, Epic Games Store, Humble Bundle, Fanatical, Green Man Gaming, and many more. We use the CheapShark API to aggregate deals from all major PC game retailers.

How accurate are the prices?

Prices are fetched from the CheapShark API and updated regularly. However, prices and deals can change at any time. Always verify the actual price on the store's website before making a purchase.

How do I add a game to my watchlist?

When viewing a game's details, tap the "Add to Watchlist" button. You can also set a target price - we'll notify you when the game drops to or below your target.

How do price alerts work?

When you add a game to your watchlist, you can set a target price. BeatPrix periodically checks prices and sends you a notification when the game reaches your target. Make sure notifications are enabled in your device settings.

Why am I not receiving notifications?

Check that notifications are enabled for BeatPrix in your device settings. On iOS, go to Settings > BeatPrix > Notifications. On Android, go to Settings > Apps > BeatPrix > Notifications. Also ensure background app refresh is enabled.

Does BeatPrix support console games?

Currently, BeatPrix focuses on PC game deals from digital stores. Console game support may be added in future updates.

How much does BeatPrix cost?

BeatPrix offers a free tier with 3 searches per day! Then just €2.99/month or €19.99/year (only €1.67/month - save 44%!) for unlimited searches, unlimited price alerts, full price history, and all premium features.

How do I sign in to sync my data?

BeatPrix supports Sign in with Apple and Sign in with Google. Simply tap the sign-in button on the onboarding screen or in Settings to create an account and sync your watchlist across devices.

How do I delete my data?

Your data (watchlist, price alerts) is stored locally on your device. To delete all data, clear the app's data in your device settings or uninstall the app. If you're signed in, you can delete your account in Settings to remove all synced data.

Can I suggest a feature?

Absolutely! We love hearing from gamers. Send your feature suggestions to support@beatprix.com and we'll consider them for future updates.
